Check Your Inbox

To complete the reset process, check your inbox for an email from trezor.io and follow the included instructions.

If you do not receive a reset email, please try the following:

  • Give it a few more minutes to reach your inbox.
  • Check your spam or junk mail folder.

If you've tried the above and still haven't received an email from us, please contact Trezor Support.
