Check Your Inbox

To complete the registration process, check your inbox for an email from trezor.io and follow the included instructions.

If you do not receive a registration email, please try the following:

  • Give it a few more minutes to reach your inbox.
  • Check your spam or junk mail folder.
  • If you already have an account registered to this email address, you can reset the account.

If you've tried the above and still haven't received an email from us, please contact Trezor Support.
